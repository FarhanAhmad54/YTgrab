Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to YT Grab ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our YouTube video downloading service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • URL Data: When you paste a YouTube URL, we process this information temporarily to fetch video details and enable downloads. This data is not stored permanently.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views, feature usage, and general traffic patterns to improve our service.
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your preferences (such as preferred quality and format) for your convenience. This data remains on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment or financial information
  • YouTube account credentials
  • Downloaded video content or viewing history
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Processing your download requests
  • Improving our service and user experience
  • Maintaining the security and functionality of our website

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our servers use secure connections (HTTPS) to encrypt data in transit.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our service
  • Content Delivery: To ensure fast and reliable access to our website

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies essential for the operation of our website. These cookies do not track you across other websites and are used only to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Maintain session functionality

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the website.

Your Rights

Since we collect minimal data and do not maintain user accounts, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your local storage preferences can be cleared through your browser settings at any time.

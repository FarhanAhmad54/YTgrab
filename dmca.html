DMCA Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Digital Millennium Copyright Act Notice

YT Grab respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998 ("DMCA"), we will respond expeditiously to claims of copyright infringement that are reported to us.

Our Position on Copyright

YT Grab is a service that provides users with the technical ability to download publicly available YouTube videos. We want to make our position clear:

  • We do not host any video content on our servers
  • We do not store downloaded videos - all content is directly streamed from source to user
  • We encourage legal use only - users are responsible for ensuring their downloads comply with copyright laws
  • We support content creators and urge users to support their favorite creators

⚠️ Important: Users are solely responsible for ensuring they have the right to download any content. Downloading copyrighted material without permission may be illegal in your jurisdiction.

Filing a DMCA Complaint

If you are a copyright owner, or authorized to act on behalf of one, and you believe that copyrighted work has been infringed through our service, please submit a DMCA takedown notice containing the following information:

Required Information for DMCA Notice

  1. Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  3. Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, including specific URLs or descriptions
  4. Your contact information (name, address, telephone number, email address)
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner
  6. A statement that the information in the notice is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Counter-Notification

If you believe that your content was mistakenly removed due to a DMCA notice, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the material that was removed and its location before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
  • Your name, address, and telephone number
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district
  • A statement that you will accept service of process from the complainant

Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA, we will terminate access to our service for users who are repeat infringers in appropriate circumstances.

Misrepresentation Warning

Under Section 512(f) of the DMCA, any person who knowingly misrepresents that material is infringing, or that material was removed by mistake, may be subject to liability for damages, including costs and attorney's fees.

Disclaimer

YT Grab provides this service for personal, non-commercial use. We do not encourage or endorse the downloading of copyrighted content without proper authorization. Users should:

  • Only download content they own or have permission to download
  • Download content that is in the public domain
  • Download content under fair use provisions
  • Support content creators by subscribing and engaging with their channels

Contact for DMCA Notices

DMCA notices and counter-notifications should be sent through our website contact methods. Please include "DMCA Notice" in your subject line for prompt handling.

Response Time

We aim to respond to valid DMCA notices within 24-48 hours of receipt. Upon receiving a valid notice, we will:

  • Review the claim for completeness
  • Take appropriate action as required by law
  • Notify the affected user if applicable
  • Maintain records of the notice and actions taken

Good Faith

We operate our service in good faith and believe in the balance between protecting copyright holders' rights and providing a useful service for legitimate personal use. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in maintaining this balance.
